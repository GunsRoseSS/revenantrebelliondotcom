We are The Revenant Rebellion

Who are we?

The Revenant Rebellion is a gaming community mainly based in The Netherlands, but has members of many European countries. We like to play games together, for example League of Legends. The community was founded by a group of friends to start a (very much) amateur League of Legends team in 2017. Since then we have experienced a ton of growth, with a discord server of over 30 active members. Our discord server is private. However, we do organise some events every now and then. Those will be posted on this site.

Tournament

We are currently organising a League of Legends tournament. It will take place around July/August in 2025 and will be played in a single weekend. Amateur teams should be looking forward for some good fun and proper banter while playing! However, we are still in the early stage of setting up the tournament and things are still subject to change. Stay tuned!
